Go4lage

Go4Lage is the ideal platform for launching small to medium-sized projects. Currently it is ready to rock 💪 as your backend for CSR projects.

Developed in Go, it offers simplicity and exceptional speed. With Golang's concurrent capabilities, you can solve many problems more elegantly than in non-parallel languages, significantly reducing complexity. Designed for self-hosting within a Docker Compose network, Go4Lage provides substantial cost savings for your company. This framework ensures that even smaller businesses can compete with the industry giants. It levels the playing field for everyone.

Its goal is to provide 80% of all batteries included at 20% of the effort.

Go4lage is not only free of charge and open source, but also completely vendor-free.
