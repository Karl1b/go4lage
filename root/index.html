Welcome to Go4lage

Go4lage is your ideal platform for launching small to medium-sized projects. Ready to power your backend for Client-Side Rendering (CSR) projects, it's time to rock your development! 💪

Why Choose Go4lage?

  • Simplicity & Speed: Developed in Go, offering exceptional performance and elegance.
  • Concurrent Capabilities: Solve complex problems more efficiently than in non-concurrent languages.
  • Cost-Effective: Designed for self-hosting within a Docker Compose network, providing substantial savings.
  • Leveling the Playing Field: Empowering smaller businesses to compete with industry giants.
  • Batteries Included: Providing 80% of essential features with just 20% of the effort.
  • Open & Free: Not only free of charge and open source but also completely vendor-free.

Go4lage is Perfect for You If:

  • You have a small team of 1-5 people working on the code.
  • You prefer self-hosting your backend instead of relying on cloud services like Firebase, Azure, or AWS.
  • You're competent and willing to learn Golang and SQL.
  • Your app needs a headless backend (CSR - Client Side Rendering).
  • Your app targets a user base under a quarter million, not requiring global-scale infrastructure.
